Fuxi Wen (M’09–SM’19) received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2013. He is an Associate Professor (research-track) in Intelligent Vehicles and Smart Mobility with the School of Vehicle and Mobility at Tsinghua University, China since 2020. Before joining Tsinghua, he held research positions in Singapore and the United Kingdom, from 2013 to 2017. He was a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellow and Researcher with the Department of Electrical Engineering, Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden, from 2017 to 2020. He has served as the Secretary of the IEEE Signal Processing Society, Sweden Chapter, from 2018 to 2020. His research interests include Intelligent Vehicular Communications and Data-Driven Automated Driving Technology.
