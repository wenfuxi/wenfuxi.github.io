Research Team

🎖 Our mission is to develop and analyze novel algorithmic solutions to current and future challenges in information fusion and vehicular communications.

  1. We are curious and challenge ourselves by actively seeking feedback through collaboration and open sharing of ideas.

  2. Our research combines theoretical analysis, the design of practical algorithms, and experimentation.

  3. We aim to generate useful knowledge and disseminate it through top-tier journal and conference publications, with high regard to both style and content.

  4. We are committed to reproducible research.
